The Nightingale of India (1929 - 2022)

Biography

Lata Mangeshkar, born on 28 September 1929, was an Indian playback singer and occasional composer. She is considered one of the greatest and most influential singers in the history of Indian music. Over her career spanning eight decades, she recorded songs in over thirty-six Indian languages and a few foreign languages.

Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna (2001)
  • Padma Vibhushan (1999)
  • Dadasaheb Phalke Award (1989)
  • Guinness World Record for most recorded songs

Famous Songs

  • Lag Ja Gale
  • Ajeeb Dastan Hai Yeh
  • Tere Bina Zindagi Se Koi Shikwa
  • Pyar Kiya To Darna Kya
